About Aquafox

Aquafox is a community-driven project focused on providing a usable web browsing solution for PowerPC Mac users.

When I returned to the PowerPC Mac scene after a hiatus, I discovered that our beloved TenFourFox, which we all relied on, had been discontinued, and not only that, but InterWebPPC had also ceased development. This left a void in the PowerPC browser landscape. After studying wicknick's commit history, I decided to pick up the torch and do exactly what InterWebPPC intended to do: release (rebranded) builds of TenFourFox whenever any "hobby mode" changes were made to the TenFourFox codebase. Since then, Aquafox has grown into an independent fork, receiving updates separately from TenFourFox.

If you're interested in reading more, you can check out the articles "Introducing Aquafox: A New Hope" and "Aquafox 2 and the Future of This Browser" on my personal website.
