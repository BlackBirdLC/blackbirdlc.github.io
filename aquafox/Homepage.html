Welcome to the Aquafox homepage!

You're probably here because you want to browse the web on your old PowerPC Mac. Well, that's exactly what Aquafox can do. Aquafox is a web browser designed for PowerPC Macs running Mac OS X. Built on the foundation of the legendary TenFourFox, Aquafox aims to follow in its footsteps to provide a functional browsing experience on these old Macs. While it may not match the speed and capabilities of modern browsers, Aquafox will do its best to deliver the best web browsing experience on your Mac!
