Key Features of Aquafox

Lightweight Design:

I'm constantly working on optimizing Aquafox by removing deprecated services and unnecessary bloat, in an attempt to make the browser as lightweight as possible.

FoxPEP Tweaks:

Aquafox inherits the majority of the enhancements from the FoxPEP project for improved performance and usability, with a few settings tweaked to provide a better out-of-the-box experience. But please, feel free to head over to about:config and make it your own.

InterWebPPC Changes:

Aquafox started as a continuation of InterWebPPC and incorporates most of the optimizations made by wicknix to the TenFourFox source code.

Security:

New releases with updated security components are issued quarterly, based on the current Firefox ESR release, to ensure compatibility with HTTPS websites.
